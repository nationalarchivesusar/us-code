# Public Law 2-6 — Shelby-Kabob Act of 2021

## Disposition
Resolved with no new current Title 18 operation.

## Review
Public Law 2-6 directed creation of 18 U.S.C. §§ 120 and 250 for unlawful taser discharge and abuse of power by a federal agent. Public Law 14-89, § 3, later repealed Public Law 2-6 in its entirety. Public Law 14-89, §§ 4 and 6, enacted replacement misconduct and force provisions and assigned them to its own Title 1, chapter 1000 structure.

The later Act did not direct restoration of any displaced preexisting text. Accordingly, no prior text is revived by the repeal. But inserting the superseded 2021 offenses into current Title 18 now would recreate a scheme Congress expressly replaced.

## Current treatment
No §§ 120 or 250 are added to Title 18. Public Law 14-89 should be handled, if desired, during the standalone-law phase because its chosen codification structure is outside the ordinary Title 18 amendment chain.
